FPS Animation Baker

A specialized tool for indie developers to bridge the gap between character assets and high-quality first-person animations. Stop manually editing meshes and start baking movements directly inside Unity.

Core Solutions

  • Instant FPS Arm Extraction: Generates a dedicated FPS arm rig from humanoid or custom characters in seconds.
  • Universal Animation Retargeting: Bake movements from existing FPS libraries directly onto your custom character bones.
  • The IK Bridge: Built-in IK solver ensures hands land exactly where they need to be on the weapon.
  • Unity-Centric Workflow: Real-time handles in Scene View for adjusting grips and hand positions during Play Mode.
