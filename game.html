About The Developer

Hello! I'm Lulucat, an indie mobile game developer passionate about creating fun, polished experiences that players can enjoy anywhere.

I focus on 2D games with clean mechanics, appealing visuals, and satisfying gameplay loops. When I'm not coding, you can find me sketching new game ideas or studying player feedback to make my games better.

My development stack includes Unity, C#, and various mobile SDKs. I handle all aspects of development from design and programming to publishing and marketing.
